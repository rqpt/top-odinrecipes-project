Microwave Oats

Description

This method is my preferred way to make and enjoy oats. As a warning, I don't beleive this to be a particularly healthy way to prepare oats. I'ts tasty though- lots of sugar, butter and syrup.

I'ts really quick and I enjoy the flow of making it in the morning.

Ingredients

  • Oats- rolled
  • Butter- margarine also works
  • Caramel Syrup- or any other kind.
  • Brown Sugar- or white..I just prefer brown.
  • Cinnamon- mix in with the sugar.
  • Milk- just a little bit
  • Salt- so the oats don't end up bland

Steps

  1. Pour half a cup of dry oats into a deep microwavable bowl.
  2. Add a pinch of salt.
  3. Add a full cup of boiling water.
  4. Add a wooden spoon to the bowl and stir.
  5. Put the bowl into the microwave on high for 3 minutes, stirring every 1 minute.
  6. Add a tablespoon of butter including the spoon itself- this will be your eating spoon.
  7. Generously season the oats with brown sugar and cinnamon.
  8. Add some dry oats to the top for extra texture.
  9. Add a few strings of syrup.
  10. Pour a little bit of milk into the bowl.
  11. Let your meal stand for a minute.
  12. Enjoy!
