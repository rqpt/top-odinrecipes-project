Stir Fry

Description

With this dish, you can just chuck whatever you have on hand into the pan. As long as there is a protein and a veggie, I'm happy and you've succeeded in making a stir fry.

If you're really adventurous you can add some fruitiness to the mix. I'm going to show you how I make mine.

  • Jam- I'm not sure which, but my wife chucks a lil bit into the pan when she comes around the kitchen. I'm pretty sure it doesn't matter which kind.

Steps

  1. Pour some frozen veggies out of the pack into the pan.
  2. Blast the heat on full power.
  3. Stir the vegies every 10 seconds or so, so they get evenly distributed around the pan.
  4. Add the beef strips after about 7 min of cooking
  5. Once the carrots are soft, you can add the jam and marmite and keep stirring.
  6. Once the beef is medium rarity its time to turn down the heat to a simmer for 3 minutes with the lid on.
  7. Serve immediately.
