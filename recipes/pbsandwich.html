Peanut Butter Sandwich

Description

This simple, yet elegant sandwich has pleasured many a tongue. Some would say, "Where is the J"? To them I say they are but fools.

The premise is simple-

Ingredients

  • Bread- the staple of many a thriving ancient civilization
  • Butter- the cornerstone of a moist, delicious sandwich
  • Peanutbutter- the underrated keto cheat snack

Together, these common ingredients combine into something truly special.

Steps

  1. Take out two slices of brown bread. (It has to be brown, it is tradition)
  2. Slather butter generously on both slices
  3. Slather peanutbutter on one slice
  4. Press slices together
  5. Slice diagonally
